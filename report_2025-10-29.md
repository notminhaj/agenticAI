### 1) [2504.16153v1] Leveraging Social Media Analytics for Sustainability Trend Detection in Saudi Arabias Evolving Market
https://arxiv.org/abs/2504.16153v1

**Problem:** The rapid economic growth and social changes in Saudi Arabia under Vision 2030 necessitate a method to detect and monitor emerging sustainability trends in real time, which can influence business and investment decisions.

**Approach:** The paper employs AI-driven methodologies to analyze millions of social media posts, news articles, and blogs. This analysis aims to identify and track sustainability trends across various sectors, including construction, food, tourism, and technology.

**Key Results:** The findings provide sector-specific and cross-sector insights, enabling decision-makers to understand market shifts in Saudi Arabia. The framework demonstrates adaptability for use in other regions, showcasing its broader applicability.

**Why It Matters:** By leveraging AI and social media analytics, the approach offers a reliable method for economists, businesses, and governments to gauge public perception and adoption of sustainability initiatives, ultimately guiding better decision-making in a rapidly evolving market.

### 2) [2502.16871v1] Utilizing Social Media Analytics to Detect Trends in Saudi Arabias Evolving Market
https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.16871v1

**Problem:** The paper addresses the challenge of identifying and tracking emerging market trends in Saudi Arabia amidst rapid economic growth and societal changes under Vision 2030. Businesses require timely insights to inform their strategies in various sectors.

**Approach:** The authors developed an AI-driven methodology that analyzes millions of social media posts monthly. This approach classifies discussions and calculates trend scores, enabling the prediction of emerging and diminishing trends across sectors like construction, technology, and entertainment.

**Key Results:** The study successfully identified key emerging trends and highlighted diminishing ones, demonstrating the effectiveness of social media analytics in trend detection. The methodology proved adaptable for use in other regions, enhancing its applicability.

**Why It Matters:** Ongoing AI-powered trend analysis can significantly improve data-informed decision-making for businesses and development strategies in a rapidly changing environment, ultimately fostering new investment opportunities and economic growth in Saudi Arabia and beyond.

### 3) [2502.16740v1] I am not thinking anymore, just following the path.: Investigating Task Delegation Trend of Author-AI Co-Creation with Generative AIs
https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.16740v1

**Problem:** The paper explores how digital comic authors delegate tasks to generative AIs during the creation process, focusing on the extent of this delegation and its effects on authors' creative agency.

**Approach:** Researchers observed 16 digital comic authors during the drafting stage, categorizing their levels of task delegation to AIs. They analyzed the variations in AI usage and how authors adjusted their delegation over time.

**Key Results:** Most authors significantly delegated tasks to AI, which correlated with reduced creation time and more detailed queries to the AI. Post-creation, about 60% of authors recalibrated their delegation levels, often opting for less delegation due to concerns over agency and AI originality.

**Why It Matters:** The findings offer insights into effective author-AI collaboration, suggesting strategies for balancing task delegation and redefining trust in co-creation. This research has implications for enhancing creative processes and understanding human-AI interactions in artistic domains.
