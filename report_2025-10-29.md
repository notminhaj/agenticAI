### 1) [2504.16153v1] Leveraging Social Media Analytics for Sustainability Trend Detection in Saudi Arabias Evolving Market

**Summary of AI Paper: Leveraging Social Media Analytics for Sustainability Trend Detection in Saudi Arabia's Evolving Market**

**Problem:** The rapid economic growth and social changes in Saudi Arabia under Vision 2030 create a need to track emerging trends, particularly in sustainability, to inform business and investment decisions.

**Approach:** The paper introduces an AI-driven methodology that analyzes millions of social media posts, news articles, and blogs to detect sustainability trends across various sectors, including construction, food and beverages, tourism, technology, and entertainment.

**Key Results:** The findings provide both sector-specific and cross-sector insights, enabling decision-makers to understand market shifts in real-time. The framework shows potential applicability beyond Saudi Arabia, suggesting broader relevance for trend detection in other regions.

**Why It Matters:** By leveraging AI and social media analytics, this research offers a reliable method for economists, businesses, and governments to gauge public perception and adoption of sustainability initiatives, ultimately supporting informed decision-making in the context of evolving market dynamics.

### 2) [2502.16871v1] Utilizing Social Media Analytics to Detect Trends in Saudi Arabias Evolving Market
https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.16871v1

The paper "Utilizing Social Media Analytics to Detect Trends in Saudi Arabia's Evolving Market" addresses the challenge of identifying emerging market trends in Saudi Arabia, particularly in the context of the country's rapid economic growth under Vision 2030. The authors propose a novel approach that leverages AI and social media analytics to analyze millions of social media posts monthly, classifying discussions and tracking trends across various sectors such as construction, food and beverage, tourism, technology, and entertainment.

Key results indicate that the methodology not only identifies emerging trends but also highlights diminishing ones, enabling businesses to make informed decisions. The AI-driven analysis provides predictive insights into trend emergence and growth, showcasing its adaptability for other regions.

This research is significant as it illustrates how ongoing AI-powered trend analysis can enhance business strategies in a dynamic market environment, ultimately fostering new investment opportunities in Saudi Arabia. By utilizing social media data, the approach offers a scalable solution for businesses seeking to navigate and capitalize on changing market landscapes.

### 3) [2502.16740v1] I am not thinking anymore, just following the path.: Investigating Task Delegation Trend of Author-AI Co-Creation with Generative AIs
https://arxiv.org/abs/2502.16740v1

**Problem:** The paper investigates how digital comic authors delegate tasks to generative AIs during the creation process, particularly focusing on the effects of this delegation on the authors' creative agency and output.

**Approach:** The authors observed 16 digital comic creators using generative AIs in their drafting stages. They categorized the levels of task delegation and analyzed the variations in AI usage, the extent of delegation, and how authors adjusted their use of AI after the co-creation process.

**Key Results:** The study found that most authors delegated significant tasks to the AI, which correlated with reduced time spent on creation and more specific queries directed at the AI. Post-co-creation, about 60% of authors recalibrated their delegation levels, often opting for less delegation due to concerns over diminished agency and the AI's lack of originality.

**Why It Matters:** This research sheds light on the evolving dynamics of human-AI collaboration in creative fields, offering strategies for authors to balance task delegation and maintain creative control. It also proposes new metrics for assessing trust in human-AI co-creation, enhancing understanding of collaborative processes in digital content creation.
